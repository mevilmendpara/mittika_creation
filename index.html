Our Story

Founded in 2025 in Bhavnagar, Gujarat, Mittika Creation was born from a simple yet powerful dream — to build a brand that makes sustainability accessible to everyone. What began as a small initiative to create eco-friendly paper cups has grown into a passionate team of 10, bringing together manufacturing, branding, and sales under one roof.

At Mittika Creation, we believe that going green shouldn’t come at a premium. Guided by our motto — “Green Cups That Shine, Prices That Delight!” 🌿☕ — we strive to deliver high-quality, affordable, and biodegradable paper cups that combine durability with design.

Our vision is simple: to see every person, business, and event choose Mittika Cups as a step toward a cleaner, greener future. With every cup we make, we move closer to making sustainability a part of everyday life.
